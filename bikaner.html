Bikaner, Rajasthan

The Desert Jewel - Camel Country

28°C • Sunny

Overview

Bikaner, a vibrant desert city in Rajasthan, is known for its magnificent forts, opulent palaces, and rich cultural heritage. Founded in 1488 by Rao Bika, it flourished as a trade center on the ancient caravan routes. The city is famous for its camel breeding farm, intricate havelis, and delectable snacks like Bikaneri bhujia. With its golden sand dunes, medieval architecture, and warm hospitality, Bikaner offers an authentic Rajasthani experience away from the usual tourist trails.

How to Reach Bikaner

30 min from airport

By Air

  • Nal Airport (BKB) - 15km from city
  • Direct flights from Delhi, Jaipur, Mumbai
  • International connections via Delhi/Mumbai
  • Major airlines: IndiGo, Air India
  • Pre-paid taxis available (₹300-500 to city center)
6-7 hrs from Delhi

By Train

  • Bikaner Junction (BKN)
  • Direct trains from Delhi (Bikaner Express - 7.5 hrs)
  • Luxury option: Palace on Wheels
  • Other important trains: Ranikhet Express, Bikaner Mail
  • Auto-rickshaws & taxis available outside station
8-9 hrs from Delhi

By Road

  • Well-connected via NH11 and NH62
  • Delhi-Bikaner via Jaipur, Sikar
  • Volvo/AC buses from Delhi (₹800-1200 per person)
  • Private taxi options (₹5000-7000 one way)
  • Major bus operators: Rajasthan Roadways, RSRTC

Bikaner's Royal Cuisine

Dal Baati Churma

Rajasthan's signature dish - baked wheat balls (baati) served with lentils (dal) and sweet crumbled wheat (churma).

Best at: Bhikharam Chandmal

Bikaneri Bhujia

Crunchy snack made from moth lentils and spices. Bikaner's most famous export since 1877.

Best at: Haldiram's

Bikaneri Rasgulla

Unique white spongy balls made from camel milk, softer than Bengali rasgullas.

Best at: Karni Sweet Home

Gatte Ki Sabzi

Gram flour dumplings cooked in spicy yogurt-based gravy, typically served with bajra roti.

Best at: Gallops Restaurant

Laal Maas

Fiery mutton curry made with Mathania red chilies, garlic and yogurt. Traditionally cooked in ghee.

Best at: Laxmi Niwas Palace

Mawa Kachori

Sweet version of kachori stuffed with mawa (reduced milk), nuts and cardamom, deep-fried and dipped in syrup.

Best at: Chotu Motu Joshi Sweet Shop

Best Time to Visit

Winter

Spring

20°C - 35°C
Mar - Apr

Summer

28°C - 45°C

Monsoon
