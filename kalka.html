Kalka-Shimla Toy Train

UNESCO World Heritage Site

Experience one of India's most scenic rail journeys through the Himalayan foothills

Journey Highlights

  • 96km scenic route through Himalayan foothills
  • Passes through 102 tunnels (longest is 1.1km)
  • Crosses 864 bridges (highest at 18.29m)
  • Lush forests of pine, oak and rhododendron
  • 5-6 hour journey time (avg speed 25km/h)

Travel Tips

  • Book tickets 2-3 months in advance for Shivalik Deluxe
  • Right side seats offer better views when traveling uphill
  • Carry snacks/water as food options are limited onboard
  • Winter journeys (Dec-Feb) offer magical snowy landscapes
  • Combine with Shimla sightseeing by arriving before noon
