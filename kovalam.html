Kovalam, Kerala's Beach Paradise

Where Golden Sands Meet the Arabian Sea in Perfect Harmony

31°C • Sunny

Overview

Kovalam, the beach paradise of Kerala, is famous for its crescent-shaped coastline dotted with palm-fringed beaches and rocky promontories. This former fishing village transformed into one of India's most popular beach destinations, known for its three beautiful beaches - Lighthouse Beach, Hawa Beach, and Samudra Beach. Kovalam offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure with its Ayurvedic massage centers, water sports, seafood shacks, and spectacular sunsets. The iconic red-and-white striped lighthouse offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, while the beachfront is lined with resorts, yoga centers, and souvenir shops.

How to Reach Kovalam

30 min from airport

By Air

  • Nearest airport: Trivandrum International (TRV)
  • Direct flights from major Indian cities & Middle East
  • International connections from Colombo, Dubai, etc.
  • Pre-paid taxis available (₹500-800 to Kovalam)
  • Airport shuttle buses to city center
1 hr from station

By Train

  • Nearest station: Trivandrum Central (TVC)
  • Rajdhani Express from Delhi (46 hrs)
  • Other important trains: Kerala Express, Trivandrum Mail
  • Local trains from nearby cities
  • Auto-rickshaws & taxis available outside station
30-45 min from city

By Road

  • Well-connected via NH66 and local roads
  • Trivandrum to Kovalam: 16km (30-45 mins)
  • KSRTC buses every 15 mins (₹20-30)
  • Private AC buses available (₹50-100)
  • Self-drive options from Trivandrum

Must-Visit Attractions

Top Rated

Lighthouse Beach

The most popular of Kovalam's beaches, named after the iconic 35m-high lighthouse. Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and spectacular sunset views from the lighthouse tower.

4.8 (7,420 reviews)
20 (Lighthouse entry)
Iconic

Kovalam Lighthouse

Built in 1972, this red-and-white striped lighthouse offers panoramic views of the Arabian Sea and Kovalam's coastline from its observation deck.

4.7 (6,130 reviews)
20 (Entry fee)
Beach

Hawa Beach (Eve's Beach)

Famous for its gentle waves and clean sands, this beach got its name from the breeze that constantly blows here. Popular for swimming and sunbathing.

4.6 (5,210 reviews)
Free Entry

Samudra Beach

The northernmost and least crowded of Kovalam's beaches, lined with luxury resorts and offering a more peaceful beach experience.

4.5 (4,180 reviews)
Free Entry

Vizhinjam Fishing Harbor

Witness the bustling activity of local fishermen bringing in their catch. Visit early morning to see the fish auction and colorful boats.

Poovar Island

Beautiful island near Kovalam where the river meets the sea, accessible by boat. Enjoy backwater cruises and pristine beaches.

Kovalam's Flavorful Cuisine

Kerala Fish Curry

Tangy and spicy fish cooked in coconut milk with kokum and traditional spices, served with steamed rice.

Best at: Fusion Bay

Avial

Mixed vegetables cooked with coconut, yogurt and curry leaves, a staple in Kerala sadya (feast).

Best at: Malabar Cafe

Kallummakkaya

Mussels cooked in spicy coconut gravy, a local delicacy best enjoyed with appam or rice.

Best at: Fishers Restaurant

Kerala Chicken Roast

Spicy roasted chicken cooked with shallots, garlic and traditional Kerala spices.

Best at: Curry Leaf

Palada Payasam

Rich rice pudding made with milk, sugar, and rice ada, traditionally served during Onam festival.

Best at: Suprabhatham

Appam with Stew

Lacy rice pancakes served with mild coconut milk-based vegetable or meat stew, a breakfast favorite.

Best at: Sreevalsam
