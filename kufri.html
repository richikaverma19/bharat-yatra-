Kufri, Snow Paradise

Himalayan Adventure and Winter Wonderland

8°C • Snowy

Overview

Kufri, located just 16km from Shimla at an altitude of 2,510 meters, is a picturesque hill station known for its breathtaking Himalayan views and winter sports. This small town transforms into a snow paradise during winter, offering excellent skiing opportunities. The Himalayan Nature Park here houses rare species like the snow leopard and Himalayan monal. Kufri's charm lies in its serene environment, apple orchards, and adventure activities. The famous Mahasu Peak offers panoramic views of Badrinath and Kedarnath peaks on clear days. Kufri's colonial-era architecture and proximity to Shimla make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Must-Visit Attractions in Kufri

Adventure

Mahasu Peak

The highest point in Kufri accessible by horseback or hike, offering panoramic views of snow-capped Himalayan peaks including Badrinath and Kedarnath on clear days.

Kufri's Flavorful Cuisine

Siddu

Traditional Himachali steamed bread made with wheat flour and stuffed with poppy seeds, walnuts or paneer, served with ghee.

Best at: Himachali Rasoi

Chha Gosht

Tender lamb cooked in yogurt-based gravy with aromatic spices, a Himachali specialty often served with rice or roti.

Best at: Kufri Resort Restaurant

Madra

Creamy chickpea or kidney bean curry cooked in yogurt with aromatic spices, a festive dish in Himachali cuisine.

Best at: Mahasu View Cafe

Momos

Tibetan-style dumplings stuffed with vegetables or meat, served with fiery red chutney, a Kufri street food staple.

Best at: Kufri Market stalls

Babru

Himachali version of kachori - deep-fried bread stuffed with black gram paste, served with tamarind chutney.

Best at: Kufri Sweet House

Thukpa

Hearty Tibetan noodle soup with vegetables, perfect for Kufri's chilly weather. Often served with chili sauce.

Best at: Snow View Restaurant
